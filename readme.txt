== Description ==

An easy way to place car or truck sales pages with content on your Wordpress site.

###What you get when you use the Car Sales pages

*  Create an unlimited amount of car or truck sales pages
*  Description field
*  Engine/Drive train field
*  Interior field
*  Exterior field
*  Saftey Features field
*  Pricing field
*  Call to Action


###Quick Start Guide

* [car slaes pages quick start guide at thinklandingpages.com] (http://www.thinklandingpages.com/car-sales-pages/)


== Installation ==


1. Upload `car sales pages` to the `/wp-content/car sales pages/` directory
1. Activate the car sales pages through the 'Car Sales Pages' menu in WordPress
1. Click **Car Sales Pages** on the admin menu to enable and set your options.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I have to know how to program or design? =

No.  The car sales pages plugin does the programming and design.

= Is there a limit to the number of car sales pages I can create? =
No, unlimited car sales pages can be created.
